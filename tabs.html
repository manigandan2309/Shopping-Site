COOLPAD

Coolpad is among the largest smartphone companies from China. It recently entered the Indian market via a partnership with the online shopping website Snapdeal.
The smartphone was launched in 7th January 2020. The phone comes with a 6.53-inch touchscreen display.
The Coolpad Legacy 5G is powered by octa-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 765 processor and it comes with 4GB of RAM.
The Coolpad Legacy 5G runs Android 10 and is powered by a 4000mAh non removable battery.

SAMSUNG

The Samsung Galaxy Tab is a line of Android-based tablet computers produced by Samsung Electronics. The Wi-Fi versions of the tablet all include a GPS system while the 3G/4G tablets include cellular capability as well.

Product lines: Samsung Galaxy Tab (Original) Manufacturer: Samsung Electronics Category: Tablet computer

IPAD

The iPad is a tablet computer developed by Apple. It is smaller than a typical laptop, but significantly larger than the average smartphone. The iPad does not include a keyboard or a trackpad, but instead has a touchscreen interface, which is used to control the device.
